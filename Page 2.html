Environmental Awareness Camps & Drives

Plastic Awareness Camps

1. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan – Plastic Awareness Camps (India)

Organizers: Government bodies, schools, colleges, NGOs

  • Cleaning drives
  • Workshops on plastic alternatives
  • Pledge-taking sessions
  • Focus: Reducing single-use plastic

2. UNEP – “Beat Plastic Pollution” Campaign

  • Youth camps and global community activities
  • Promotes recycling & sustainable packaging
  • Encourages responsible consumption

3. World Environment Day Camps

  • Beach & river clean-ups
  • Workshops & eco-exhibitions
  • Focus on waste management & marine litter

4. School & College Eco-Clubs

  • Microplastics education
  • Waste segregation
  • DIY upcycling projects
  • Promoting eco-friendly alternatives
E-Waste Awareness Drives

1. “Dump and Donate” Drive (India)

  • Conducted in 120 Indian cities
  • Collect usable electronics for donation
  • Recycle non-usable e-waste properly

2. EWRI – E-Waste Awareness Camps

  • Held in schools and communities
  • Ryan School, Rohini – Aug 2023
  • DPS Rohini – May 2023

3. RecyclEarth E-Waste Collection (Karnataka)

  • 15-day campaign (14–30 Oct 2023)
  • Collected approx. 800 kg e-waste
  • CRT TVs, mobile phones, chargers

4. IEEE ARMIET & NSS E-Waste Drive (Thane)

  • Drive: 27 March 2024
  • Rally + collection in Sapgaon
  • Expert talk on circular economy
  • 55 participants
